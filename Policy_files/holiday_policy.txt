Company Holiday Policy

The company observes a mix of national, regional, and company-declared holidays each
calendar year.

Fixed Holidays

The following holidays are observed across all company locations:

Republic Day – January 26
Independence Day – August 15
Gandhi Jayanti – October 2

Flexible Holidays

Employees may choose 2 flexible holidays per year from a list of regional or religious
holidays published annually by HR.

Weekend Policy

If a declared company holiday falls on a weekend, it will not be carried forward or
substituted unless explicitly stated by HR.

Holiday Calendar

The official holiday calendar is released at the beginning of each year and is available on the
internal HR portal.

Critical Operations Exception

Employees required to work on a company holiday due to critical operations may be granted
a compensatory off, subject to manager approval.
