Employee Leave Policy

All full-time employees are entitled to various types of leave as outlined below. Leave must
be approved by the employeeâ€™s reporting manager through the internal HR portal.

Types of Leave

Casual Leave (CL)
Employees are entitled to 12 days of casual leave per calendar year.
Casual leave can be taken for personal reasons and must be applied at least one day in
advance, except in emergencies.

Sick Leave (SL)
Employees are entitled to 10 days of sick leave per year. Sick leave of more than two
consecutive days requires submission of a valid medical certificate.

Earned Leave (EL)
Earned leave is accrued at the rate of 1.5 days per month and can be carried forward up to a
maximum of 30 days.

Leave Encashment

Unused earned leave may be encashed at the time of separation, subject to company policy
and statutory regulations.

Leave Without Pay (LWP)

If an employee exhausts all eligible leave, additional leave may be granted as Leave Without
Pay, subject to manager approval.

Special Project Exception

Employees assigned to critical business projects may be granted up to 15 days of casual
leave per year, subject to approval from senior management and HR.
